How This Generic Ad Free Word Game Works

This game works like that other word game: guess the 5-letter word in 6 tries.

There are two modes:

  • Random Mode: a new word each game, selected randomly.
  • Daily Mode: everyone gets the same daily word, deterministically, based on the day.
