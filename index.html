Projects I am working on

RoryFlix: I am making a local web app to help users save video source urls from streaming websites to watch later before the orignal website is shut down.

You get the shows straight from the source and save them to a playlist.

In Canada, including Nova Scotia, simply saving a video stream URL to a text file is not inherently illegal. URLs themselves are not protected by copyright, and recording them for personal reference does not, by itself, constitute infringement. However, the legality of this action depends on the context and intended use.
